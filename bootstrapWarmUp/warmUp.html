Inpsiring People

In this assignment, you will list inspiring people, characters, and moments that you've witnessed in your life, and explain why you appreciate them. The goal of this project is to have a page to reference when you want to feel inspired.

Fictional Characters

We will start with fictional charcaters; take a moment to think about what fictional characters you admire. These can be characters from television shows, movies, video games, or a book. The goal is to let your imagination run wild. For each character, we will do the following:

  1. First, we will find an image of the charcater. Naviagte to google and find an image that you would like to use to highlight the character that you admire. Right click on the image, and select copy image address and link it into your project code.
  2. Next, we want to highlight the charcacters name
  3. Afterwards, we will write a short description of at least three sentences about why the character inspires us
  4. Lastly, we will link our readers to a page that highlights more information about this character so that they can read more about them on their own.
